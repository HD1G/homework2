Some words about me.

Hi! My name is Ryan Hashier. Welcome to my online portfolio. I currently reside
in Cleveland, Ohio USA, and I am studying at Case Western Reserve University's Coding
Bootcamp. My education background includes a B.A. from Baldwin-Wallace University in
Business Management. I have also completed the post graduate Graphic Design program
at Cleveland State University.

My professional career and education in Graphic Design always kept me close to web
development. Especially in front end design. Whether it be helping design certain
elements for a website, or help styling a color palette on an email newsletter, I always
seemed to overlap into the world of web. I quickly learned there was more involved in
web development than just making great looking websites. That's how I ended up at CWRU's
Coding Bootcamp, with the desire to learn it all. I thank you for taking the time to view
my portfolio of work. Please contact me with any questions.
